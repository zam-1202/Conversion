CALCULATOR

A device that performs numerical arithmetic operations.
The most basic calculators can only perform addition, subtraction,
multiplication, and division. Exponential operations, roots, logarithms,
trigonometric functions, and hyperbolic functions can all be handled
by more sophisticated calculators. Internally, some calculators perform
all of these functions through repeated addition processes.
